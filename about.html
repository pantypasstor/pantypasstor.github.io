About PANTY

Brought up on the sonic transmissions of the NYC and Chicago underground, interdisciplinary artist, DJ, and producer PANTY is happy to report that hardcore will never die.

Enmeshing sonic landscapes from seemingly disparate ends of global bass & leftfield dance music into a cohesive and experimental whole, PANTY’s punk-at-heart performance antics are only enhanced by an anti-capitalist, DIY-or-die ethos, and a passion for carving out spaces for ecstatic release.
